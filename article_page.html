4 Best smartphone in the world at the moment

10:00:05 10/10/2015

This list is compiled, voted and published by Business Insider.

Apple introduced its new iPhone duo in early September, while Google recently released two of its newest Nexus smartphones. Therefore, the face of the world's best smartphone list also has certain disturbances.

1. Apple iPhone 6s

Business Insider rated iPhone 6s as the best phone at the moment. Although there are not many differences in appearance compared to its predecessor, iPhone 6s is equipped with significant upgrades in parameters - configuration, including better camera, better performance and screen technology. 3D Touch. If you prefer a large screen phone, the iPhone 6s Plus would be a better choice, but for most people 4.7 inches is a good fit.

2. Apple iPhone 6s Plus

Similar to iPhone 6s, iPhone 6s Plus was also appreciated in the large-screen phone segment, which was previously the exclusive "area" of Android smartphone Manufacturer such as Samsung. The Apple Phone is a harmonious combination of hardware, design and the variety and stability of software

3. Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge+

Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge + has the same screen size as Samsung Galaxy Note 5 and has a design similar to Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge. In terms of user experience, in addition to not having an S-Pen, the Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge + promises to be one of the best Android phablets available today.

4. HTC One (M9)

This year, the HTC One (M9) did not attract too much attention from the community of interest due to not owning many changes compared to its predecessor. However, it is undeniable that One (M9) is one of the best smartphones available today. The most notable highlight on HTC One (M9) lies in its beautiful, luxurious and sophisticated design.

(Refer: Business Insider)
